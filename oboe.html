Oboe

The oboe was one of the easier wind instruments to simulate. In this clip, we have an oboe playing a concert C4 at mezzo-forte.
For wind instruments, the attack phase is not as evident as the attack phase for brass instruments, but the wind from a musician comes not abrupt, but more deliberately. Therefore, the attack phase isn't sharp. For the oboe, flute, and clarinet, the sustain phase is as long as a musician wants to keep it for, so we chose a sustain length of .8 seconds with a release length of .2 seconds.
